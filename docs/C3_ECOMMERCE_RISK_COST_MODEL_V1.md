# E-commerce AI Governance Risk Cost Model v1

## A framework for estimating the operational cost of ungoverned AI customer support interactions

---

## Section 1: Interaction Volume Assumption

```text
Monthly AI Customer Support Interactions: 100,000
Assumption basis: Mid-scale e-commerce platform with AI-assisted customer support
```

## Section 2: High-Risk Interaction Estimate

```text
Estimated high-risk interaction rate: 1–3%
Monthly high-risk interactions: 1,000–3,000
Types: Escalation pressure, emotional volatility, coercive threats, crisis signals
```

## Section 3: Escalation Conversion

```text
Estimated human escalation rate (unmanaged): 10–20% of high-risk interactions
Monthly unmanaged escalations: 100–600
Each escalation requires: average 15–30 minutes of human review time
```

## Section 4: Operational Impact Categories

| Impact Category | Description | Estimated Frequency |
|---|---|---|
| Customer Escalation | High-risk interactions trigger formal complaints | 10–15% of high-risk |
| Support Load Increase | Repeat contacts from unresolved high-risk interactions | 20–30% repeat contact rate |
| Policy Overcommitment | AI makes promises that cannot be fulfilled | 5–10% of GUIDE-type interactions |
| Brand Risk | Threats to post publicly escalate to actual incidents | 1–3% of coercive interactions |
| Audit Risk | Inability to trace why AI responded in a specific way | 100% of ungoverned interactions |
| Human Review Cost | Manual intervention required for undetected high-risk | 100–600 cases/month |

## Section 5: Governance Value Mapping

| Without Governance | With Continuum C3 |
|---|---|
| Inconsistent escalation handling | Standardized intervention routing |
| No decision traceability | Governance evidence available for every interaction |
| AI overcommitment risk | Policy-constrained responses with enforcement trace |
| Reactive human escalation | Structured escalation thresholds with audit trail |
| Crisis signals missed | BLOCK routing with human oversight trigger |
| No audit coverage | 100% governance evidence stored |

## Section 6: Key Governance Metrics

```text
Metrics that Continuum C3 makes measurable:
- Escalation Rate: % of interactions triggering formal escalation
- Human Handoff Rate: % of interactions requiring human intervention
- High-Risk Interaction Rate: % of interactions classified as GUIDE or BLOCK
- Untraceable Intervention Rate: % of interventions without governance evidence
```

## Section 7: Summary Statement

```text
The risk is not that AI will say the wrong thing.
The risk is that AI will do the right thing — at the wrong moment — 
with no record of why it happened.

Continuum C3 makes every governance decision auditable, 
enforceable, and accountable.
```
